PAMUKKALE TOUR – SMALL GROUP
(From Kusadasi Port or Hotels)

Pamukkale Tour with Small Group. Pick up from Kusadasi Cruise Port or from hotels located in Kusadasi, Selcuk. Drive to Pamukkale. Visit Pamukkale Hierapolis, white calcified terraces, Temple of Apollo, The Theatre, Necropolis and thermal baths.



Tour Plan

  • DAY 1
  • Pamukkale Tour with Small Group
  • Tour starting point: Pick up from Kusadasi Cruise Port or Hotels from Kusadasi, Selcuk
    Tour ending point: Kusadasi Cruise Port or Hotels from Kusadasi, Selcuk

Tour Information

Sights to see : We will drive to Pamukkale which is an UNESCO World Heritage site with its white calcified terraces. Lunch on arrival. Then visit the ancient city of Hierapolis right next to the white terraces with its Temple of Apollo, the Theatre, Necropolis and the thermal baths where Cleopatra and famous Roman genaral Mark Antony stayed for honeymoon.
